OPEN QUESTIONS — GET ANSWERS BEFORE PAYING

Q1
InClips
Which specific accounts and pages will distribute the content? Can we see examples of similar casino campaigns they've run?
Q2
InClips
How are view counts verified? Is it platform-reported (self-reported) or third-party verified? What happens if the 100M view minimum is not reached?
Q3
InClips
Can GoLuna approve all content before it goes live? What's the turnaround time from brief to first post?
Q4
InClips
What is the refund or make-good policy if campaigns underperform? Is any part of the budget refundable?
Q5
MAADS
From first streamer contact, how long until the first stream goes live? And what guarantees exist that streamers deliver the agreed streams and don't take payment and disappear?
Q6
MAADS
Who owns the social accounts used for clipping? Can GoLuna/Hotshot retain the accounts after the campaign ends for long-term niche-building?
Q7
MAADS
At 10M impressions ($12.5k total), how many accounts and how many videos are produced? What about at 25M and 50M?
Q8
MAADS
What viewership ranges distinguish starter, mid-tier, and macro streamers for the proposed packages? Give us practical examples so we can evaluate quality.
