Universal Protection Protocols

01 · Tracking First

Never spend a single dollar without S2S postback tracking confirmed and firing test events. Verify your tracker registers a test conversion before any campaign goes live. Non-negotiable across all 5 networks.

02 · Start Small

RichAds: $150 test (COL or VNM Push). ROIAds: $1,500–2,000. AADS: small test per channel. BCA: $10k activation minimum only, no more until tracking confirmed. HypeLab: any amount — no minimum required.

03 · Cross-Check Clicks

Your tracker should show within 5–10% of the platform's reported clicks. If your tracker shows 100 clicks and they report 500, pause immediately and escalate. Significant discrepancy means inflated traffic.

04 · Get Everything In Writing

Any verbal promise about managed service, budget splits, KPIs, or performance guarantees must go in an email or contract before you pay. Verbal confirmation from an AM is not protection. We have been burned before.

05 · Demand Placement Reports

Within 48 hours of going live, request a placement report showing which sites your ads appeared on. BCA and RichAds are weakest on this. If they cannot provide it, pause spend and escalate to a senior contact immediately.

06 · Non-Refundable = Risk Capital

RichAds and ROIAds explicitly refuse to refund unused balances. Treat every dollar deposited as gone. Only deposit what you are genuinely prepared to lose on a test that may fail completely.
