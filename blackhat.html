Johan and David propose a long-term cooperation where GoLuna learns internal Black Hat capability while the agency supplies tech and relationships that take years to build independently. Chris wants hands-on involvement to understand the system before delegating. Agency's weakest area is creative — Chris's strongest. Natural complementarity.

Risk context
Black Hat Meta advertising violates Meta's Terms of Service. Ad accounts will be banned — this is built into the model, not a failure state. The agency and David have been burned on compliance recently — this is an active, near-term concern they flagged explicitly. Account lifespan is finite by design. The economics only work if the revenue-per-account-lifespan exceeds the cost of sourcing and running the account.

Critical mindset — don't stay frozen
Black Hat punishes slow movers because accounts die and learnings reset. If a method works, reallocate aggressively same month. The test budget is a signal-finding exercise, not a steady state. Speed of reallocation is part of the strategy.
LATAM overlap with other channels
The LATAM test markets (Argentina, Chile, Brazil) overlap with the RichAds data we already have — COL Push at $0.81 eCPA and BRA Direct Click at $2.62 registration eCPA. Black Hat Meta will run parallel to, not instead of, those channels. Deduplication and attribution clarity between channels is critical from day one.
